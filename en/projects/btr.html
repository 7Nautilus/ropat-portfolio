BTR

Album cover design project for the BTR EP by French artist Maltezz.

The goal was to create a strong visual identity that aligns with the artist's musical universe.

I worked on the graphic composition, color choices, and typography to reflect the energy and unique style of BTR.

This project allowed me to develop my graphic design skills while exploring new creative techniques.
