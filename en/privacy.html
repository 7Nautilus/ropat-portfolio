Privacy Policy

At Ropat, we take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy informs you about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is:
Ropat
Email: vultur.psd@gmail.com

2. Data Collected

2.1 Browsing Data

When you visit our site, we automatically collect certain information via Google Analytics:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and visit duration
  • Traffic source (search engine, direct link, etc.)
  • Screen resolution and language preferences

2.2 Contact Form

If you contact us by email, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (if provided)
  • The content of your message

3. Purpose of Processing

The collected data is used to:

  • Analyze site audience and improve user experience
  • Respond to your contact requests
  • Ensure site security
  • Comply with our legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of your data is based on:

  • Your consent: for analytics cookies (Google Analytics)
  • Legitimate interest: for audience analysis and site improvement
  • Contract execution: to respond to your contact requests

5. Retention Period

  • Analytics data: 26 months (maximum duration authorized by CNIL)
  • Contact emails: maximum 3 years, then deletion
  • Cookies: maximum 13 months

6. Data Recipients

Your data may be transmitted to:

  • Google Analytics: for audience analysis (hosted in the United States with GDPR guarantees)
  • Google Tag Manager: for tracking tag management
  • GitHub Pages: site host (United States)

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

7. Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your data
  • Right of rectification: correct your inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction: limit the processing of your data
  • Right to object: object to the processing of your data
  • Right to portability: retrieve your data in a structured format
  • Right to withdraw consent: at any time for cookies

To exercise these rights, contact us at: vultur.psd@gmail.com

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority: ICO (UK) or CNIL (France)

8. Cookies

8.1 What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your computer when you visit a website.

8.2 Cookies Used

This site uses the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat): audience measurement
  • Google Tag Manager: tracking tag management

8.3 Cookie Management

You can refuse cookies by configuring your browser:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

You can also use the Google Analytics Opt-out extension to disable tracking.

9. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data against loss, misuse, or alteration:

  • HTTPS encryption across the entire site
  • Secure hosting (GitHub Pages)
  • Restricted access to personal data
  • Regular security system updates

10. International Transfers

Some of your data may be transferred outside the European Union (particularly to the United States for Google Analytics and GitHub).

These transfers are governed by:

  • EU standard contractual clauses
  • Appropriate safeguards compliant with GDPR
